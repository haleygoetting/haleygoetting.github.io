B-Side

Our mission at the B-Side Arts collective is to create accessible, inclusive spaces in visual and performing arts for those from underrepresented backgrounds. We offer creative educational workshops and programs, and seek to build a community that invites students to create, imagine, and explore together.

Since my engagement with B-side over the last four years, and as Co-Chair, I've helped rebuild the community that had dissolved during the pandemic, leading workshops, and organizing various community events. B-side has become an indispensible pillar of support and inspiration in my life.
